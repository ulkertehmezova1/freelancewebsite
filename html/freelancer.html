About

Designed and built with all the love in the world by the Bootstrap team with the help of our contributors.Bootstrap is a free and open-source CSS framework directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development. It contains CSS- and (optionally) JavaScript-based design templates for typography, forms, buttons, navigation, and other interface components.

Bootstrap, originally named Twitter Baseline, was developed by Mark Otto and Jacob Thornton at Twitter as a framework to encourage consistency across internal tools.
